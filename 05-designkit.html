What DesignKit Does

Creativity meets logic.

Born from a fashion design background and built with code — DesignKit applies colour theory and typographic principles automatically so your brand looks considered, not generated.

🎨

Colour Palette Generator

Uses colour theory logic — complementary, analogous, and triadic relationships — to generate palettes that are visually harmonious, not just randomly picked.

✍️

Typography Pairing

Curated font pairings that combine a display serif with a readable sans-serif and a monospace label font — the three-font system used by professional brand designers.

📦

Exportable Brand Kit

Export your complete brand identity as a PDF, CSS variables file, or JSON token set — ready to drop into your project, Figma, or hand to a developer.

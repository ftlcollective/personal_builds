What MindBridge Offers

Small steps toward feeling better.

Mental health doesn't improve in big leaps. It improves in daily check-ins, honest reflections, and knowing that support is always within reach.

📊

Mood Tracking

Log how you feel each day in seconds. Over time, patterns emerge — helping you understand what lifts you and what drains you.

📓

Daily Journaling

A private, guided space to write. Prompts change daily to help you explore what's on your mind without knowing where to start.

🔗

Curated Resources

Hand-picked helplines, reading, and exercises — updated daily via public API integration and filtered by what you need most.

Built with empathy
Why This Exists

Technology for healing.

MindBridge was built because mental health tools are often cold, clinical, or overwhelming. This is the opposite — warm, simple, and focused on you.

The mood tracker stores data only in your browser. Nothing is sent to a server. Your journal is yours alone. This is a tool for your wellbeing, not a product harvesting your vulnerability.

This project was built as part of my full-stack development journey — combining API integration skills with a deep personal commitment to mental health advocacy.

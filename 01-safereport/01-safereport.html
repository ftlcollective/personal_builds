About SafeReport

Technology that protects.

SafeReport was built on a simple belief: that digital systems should make vulnerable people safer, not more exposed. Every design decision — from the form fields to the colour choices — was made with the survivor in mind.

No personal information is ever required. Reports are encrypted end-to-end and routed anonymously to verified support organisations. You are in control of every word you share.

This project was built as part of my journey as a full-stack developer and Women's Health Practitioner — combining technical skills with lived advocacy experience.
